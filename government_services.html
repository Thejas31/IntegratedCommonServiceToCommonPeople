Aadhar

Aadhar is a 12-digit unique identity number that can be obtained by residents of India, based on their biometric and demographic data. Apply for Aadhar

You can also update your Aadhar details such as address, mobile number, etc. through the Aadhar Self Service Update Portal.

Ration Card

A ration card is an official document issued by state governments in India to households that are eligible to purchase subsidized food grain from the Public Distribution System (PDS). Apply for Ration Card

Check the status of your ration card application online and update your details as necessary.

Passport

A passport is an official document issued by a government, certifying the holder's identity and citizenship and entitling them to travel under its protection to and from foreign countries. Apply for Passport

Renew your passport, check application status, and find information on required documents and fees.
