Aadhaar Services

Aadhar is a 12-digit unique identity number that can be obtained by residents of India, based on their biometric and demographic data.

You can also update your Aadhar details such as address, mobile number, etc. through the Aadhar Self Service Update Portal.

Ration Card Services

A ration card is an official document issued by state governments in India to households that are eligible to purchase subsidized food grain from the Public Distribution System (PDS).

Check the status of your ration card application online and update your details as necessary.

Apply for a new ration card or check the status of your application.

Passport Services

A passport is an official document issued by a government, certifying the holder's identity and citizenship and entitling them to travel under its protection to and from foreign countries.

Apply for a passport, locate Passport Seva Kendras, or consult with visa/passport consultants.

Driving License Services

Apply for a new driving license, renew your existing one, or check the status of your application through the Parivahan Sewa portal.

Vehicle Registration Services
